WELCOME TO ANRES 2018

Thongchai Suwonsichon General Chairman, ANRES 2018.

Message from the Conference Chairman

   It is a great honor and privilege for me to welcome you to the Agricultural and Natural Resources conference 2018 (ANRES 2018). I am the most delighted to address this highly prominent gathering of distinguished participants. I wish to accord a warm welcome to all of you at this conference.

   ANRES is a peer reviewed international scientific journal which publishes papers in the categories of review articles, research articles and short communications and technical notes from all areas related to agricultural disciplines. It covers original, previously unpublished research regarding any theories and practices that are related to agricultural applications. The ANRES 2018 is jointly organized by the Kasetsart University. It will be held April 26-28, 2018 at Hotel Windsor Suites & Convention, Bangkok, Thailand.

   This conference has been very well planned with the intention to provide an opportunity for all scientists, engineers and many participants strengthening and enlightening Agricultural and Natural resources science and technology for future. It is a great opportunity for all participants to fruitfully find out collaboration among researchers from different countries. I hope that this conference will provide knowledge, applications, recent advances technology and the latest research and development in fields of Agricultural and Natural Resources. On the behalf of ANRES organizing committee, we wish to see as many scientists, engineers and technologists from many countries strengthening our network and collaboration of future research work. We hope you will join us for this outstanding conference and have enough time to enjoy Thai cultures, fascinating and spectacular places, friendly Thais, extraordinary Thai cuisine and delicious fruits.

   I am looking forward to meeting you in Bangkok during ANRES2018 and to sharing most interesting knowledge and experiences. My personal respect and thanks in advance to all of you.
