Guidelines for Oral Presentation

12 min for presentation and 3 min for discussion Speaker should upload presentation file (.pptx, .pdf) before session. Oral Presentation awards will be announced at closing ceremony on 28 Apr. Awardee is requested to attend.

Guidelines for Poster Presentation
  • Poster size: 60 cm (W) X 110cm (H) Top and bottom margins 1 inch
  • Discussion time is set for poster presentations. Please check the schedule to confirm your discussion time
  • Please set your poster on the poster panel labeled with your presentation number on 26 Apr at 8.30-11.00 and removal poster on 28 Apr before 12.00.
  • Poster awards will be announced at closing ceremony on 28 Apr. Awardee is requested to attend
